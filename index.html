Why This Board?

Linear Actuation

Hand-lubricated linear switches with custom spring weights provide a buttery smooth, friction-free typing experience.

Polycarbonate Chassis

CNC-milled matte polycarbonate. Dense, weighted, and structurally rigid to provide the ultimate acoustic foundation.

Acoustic Dampening

A custom PORON foam matrix eliminates unwanted chassis resonance, delivering a deep, signature 'thock' with every keystroke.

Ambient Illumination

Crisp, per-key backlighting tuned to an icy cyan aesthetic. Fully programmable via QMK/VIA to match your workspace.
